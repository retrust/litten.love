How To Bypass iCloud Lock

For checkra1n Devices

Please visit this page on your Mac or PC.

Keep in mind that iCloud and Cellular may be broken after proceeding (a restore fixes it, but you'll be locked out again)
But it's still useful for tweak development/testing or such

Step 1:
Connect your device to your Mac and jailbreak with checkra1n and leave it connected.

Step 2:
Open a Terminal window and install homebrew by typing
/usr/bin/ruby -e "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Homebrew/install/master/install)"

Step 3:
install libimobiledevice with homebrew by typing
brew install libimobiledevice

Step 4:
Open a second terminal window (command + n) and type iproxy 2022 44 in the first one.
Type this in the second window ssh root@localhost -p 2022 and just enter your device's ssh password (default: alpine)

Step 5:
When connected with SSH in the second terminal window type mount -o rw,union,update / to mount your device.

Step 6:
Type this again in your second window (like all next steps) echo "" >> /.mount_rw

Step 7:
Now we'll delete the setup.app to bypass everything, type this
rm -rf /Applications/Setup.app

Step 8:
For the final touches we'll reload the icon cache and respring so type
uicache --all and give it a moment.

Step 9:
For the last step we'll just respring
killall backboardd
